Using Technology Safely While Learning

Technology can greatly support learning, but it‚Äôs important to use it wisely. Protect your personal data by avoiding sharing passwords or private information, especially on unknown sites or apps. Use strong passwords and keep your software updated to avoid security risks.

Take care of your health while learning online by resting your eyes, moving regularly, and sitting comfortably with good lighting. Choose trusted apps and websites recommended by teachers or with good reviews.

Remember to keep balance. Technology should help, not replace learning. Safe and mindful use will make your learning more effective and secure.

SmallTalk2Me

SmallTalk2Me is an innovative educational app powered by artificial intelligence, designed to develop speaking and writing skills in English. It allows users to record their own responses and receive instant, detailed feedback on pronunciation, grammar, fluency, and vocabulary. The app also offers exercises that prepare learners for language exams and job interviews.

Usefulness for high school students: SmallTalk2Me is a valuable tool for learning English at the upper-secondary level. It effectively supports the development of communication skills and helps boost students‚Äô confidence when using English in both everyday and formal situations.

Write and improve

Write & Improve is a free educational app developed by the University of Cambridge, aimed at helping users improve their English writing skills. It allows learners to complete writing tasks independently at various proficiency levels and receive instant feedback on grammar, vocabulary, and structure. The system evaluates texts according to the CEFR scale (A1‚ÄìC2), enabling users to track their progress over time.

Use in upper-secondary education: Write & Improve is an effective tool for high school students, especially in preparation for English exams such as the Matura or Cambridge certificates. Through automated text analysis and the ability to revise and resubmit work, the app promotes learner autonomy and supports the development of writing competence in English.

Visuwords

Visuwords is an interactive online dictionary and thesaurus that presents word meanings and their connections in the form of a colorful concept map resembling a neural network. When a word is entered, users are shown a dynamic diagram in which nodes represent different parts of speech (e.g., nouns, verbs, adjectives), and the connecting lines illustrate semantic relationships such as synonyms, antonyms, or hierarchical links.

The app uses the WordNet database developed by Princeton University, providing a rich vocabulary resource and precise definitions. This makes Visuwords especially useful for high school students, teachers, and anyone learning English, offering visual support for vocabulary expansion and a better understanding of complex word relationships.

Cambridge English ‚Äì Grammar Exercises

Cambridge English ‚Äì Grammar Exercises are free, interactive online activities developed by Cambridge English, designed to help learners study and reinforce grammar topics across various proficiency levels (from A1 to C2). They are available on the Cambridge English: Activities for Learners website, where users can filter exercises by level, skill (e.g., grammar, vocabulary), and duration.

For high school students: These exercises offer valuable support in preparing for language exams such as the Matura or Cambridge certificates (e.g., B2 First, C1 Advanced). With instant feedback, students can independently identify and correct their mistakes, making grammar practice more effective and focused on exam success.

Lingoclip

LingoClip is a language learning app that helps users improve their skills through music. Learners listen to songs and fill in missing words in the lyrics, which enhances vocabulary, pronunciation, and listening comprehension.

Available on Android, iOS, and via web browser, the app supports multiple languages. It‚Äôs an ideal tool for high school students who want to learn in a fun and engaging way.

Padlet

Padlet is an interactive online digital board that allows users to create, share, and collaboratively edit various types of content‚Äîsuch as text, images, links, videos, and files.

It is widely used in education for teamwork, brainstorming, projects, and presentations. Thanks to its simplicity and flexibility, Padlet works great in high schools, supporting student creativity and collaboration.
